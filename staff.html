Everything a moderator or server manager needs to use Astra safely and effectively.

🛡️

Moderation commands

Astra gives your server strong moderation tools for keeping communities safe and welcoming.

  • /mod warn — log warnings and DM users.
  • /mod kick — remove members cleanly with a recorded case.
  • /mod ban — ban users with optional message deletion.
  • /mod timeout — mute members for a set time.
  • /mod case — review case details quickly.
🎫

Ticket & support setup

Use Astra tickets to give your team private support channels and keep conversations organized.

  • Set up ticket channels and staff access with /ticket config.
  • Close tickets cleanly with /ticket close.
  • Keep logs secure and only visible to staff.
  • Use staff roles to control who can see ticket requests.
⚙️

Role & permissions guide

Grant the right permissions so Astra can manage staff actions without giving it extra power.

  • Allow Manage Roles for staff moderation commands.
  • Give Astra Ban Members and Kick Members if you want those tools enabled.
  • Use a dedicated staff role for clean permission separation.
  • Keep staff commands limited to trusted users only.
Best practices

Astra works best when your staff team has a clear process. Use a single moderator role, keep channel access limited, and always log moderation actions in support or audit channels.

  • Create a staff-only category for ticket channels and logs.
  • Keep the staff role separate from general admin roles.
  • Use /mod history and /mod case before taking action.
  • Explain moderation actions clearly to your members.
