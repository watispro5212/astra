Professional Moderation

Every server deserves safety. Astra provides a mission-ready toolkit for staff teams, including numbered case tracking, automated logging, and native button-based confirmations for high-stakes actions.

  • Audit Trails: Every kick, ban, and mute is logged with a unique case ID.
  • Appeal System: Integrated workflows for member appeals and case reviews.
  • Data Sovereignty: Export your server's moderation history to CSV or JSON anytime.
