## 1. 📜 THE RULES

*Post these in `#📜 rules` as a single, clean message.*

> ### 🛑 Server Rules
> 
> **1. Respect Others**
> Treat everyone with kindness. Harassment, hate speech, or bullying will result in a permanent ban.
> 
> **2. No Spamming or Advertising**
> Avoid sending too many messages, emojis, or pings. Do not advertise other servers or services without permission.
> 
> **3. Use the Right Channels**
> Only ask for bot help in the **Support** channels. This keeps general chat clean for everyone.
> 
> **4. Keep it Family Friendly**
> Astra is a community bot. All content should be safe for work (SFW) and appropriate.
> 
> **5. No Dangerous Links**
> Do not share links to scams, malware, or illegal sites. We have zero tolerance for safety risks.
> 
> **6. Listen to Staff**
> The decision of a Moderator or Admin is final. If you have a problem, open a ticket to talk to us privately.
> 
> **7. English Only**
> To make sure our staff can help everyone, please keep public conversations in English.
> 
> **8. No Impersonation**
> Do not pretend to be Astra staff, other bots, or other members. 
> 
> **9. Follow Discord Rules**
> You must follow the [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) at all times.
> 
> **10. Privacy**
> Do not share private chats or personal info about others.

## 🧩 4. ONBOARDING SETUP
*Use these settings in: Server Settings -> Onboarding.*

### Question 1: What brings you here today?
- **Option 1**: 🆘 "I need help with the Astra bot."
  - **Action**: Give `Support Seeker` role.
- **Option 2**: 🚀 "I want to stay updated on new features."
  - **Action**: Give `News Ping` role.
- **Option 3**: 🧪 "I want to help test new features."
  - **Action**: Give `Bot Tester` role.
- **Option 4**: 💬 "I just want to hang out."
  - **Action**: Give `Verified` role.
