Changelog

Track every update, feature, and improvement to Astra.

v2.1.0 April 18, 2026 Design Overhaul
  • Branding: Massive visual upgrade with the "Premium Glass" design system.
  • Typography: Introduced Outfit and Inter font families for a modern feel.
  • UI Components: Refined glassmorphism on all cards, buttons, and navbars with internal glows.
  • Navigation: Added a dedicated "Changelog" page accessible via the global navbar.
  • Optimizations: Smoother animations and refined border-hover effects across the site.
v2.0.0 April 18, 2026 Major Feature Update
  • Engagement: Comprehensive XP and Leveling system with leaderboard and role rewards.
  • Giveaways: Button-based giveaways with persistent state and automated rerolls.
  • Auto-Moderation: Advanced filters for spam, links, invites, and excessive caps.
  • Moderation: Added /mute, /unmute, /unban, and /purge commands.
  • Utilities: "Join-to-Create" temporary voice channels and /stats dashboards.
  • Tickets v2: Categorized support types and automated HTML transcript logging.
