Quick Start

Deploying Astra to your server is a zero-config process. Most features are autonomous and activate the moment the bot joins your server.

/info stats

Execute the command above to verify system integrity once Astra is present in your member list.

Inviting Astra

To establish a link with Astra, use the official invite portal. We recommend granting Administrator permissions for the most seamless experience across all 12 tactical sectors.

  • › Global Slash Command access required
  • › Automatic channel/role management
  • › High-priority message processing

Industrial Finance

Astra's fiscal ecosystem is divided into two primary sectors: the Industrial Hub (Shop) and the ATX Stock Market.

/economy stats
/stockmarket list

Administrators can configure shop items and roles via the /shop protocol, while the ATX Index operates autonomously, simulating a high-volatility financial environment for all operatives.
