TACTICAL PROTOCOLS

The complete Astra v7.3.0 Omega command registry — every slash command, its permission tier, and what it does.

🛡️ Moderation Sector
/mod kick
Eject a member from the sector with an optional reason. Respects role hierarchy.
Staff
/mod ban
Permanently blacklist a member. Optionally delete up to 7 days of their messages.
Staff
/mod unban
Revoke an active ban by user ID. Logs an unban case automatically.
Staff New
/mod timeout
Apply a timed suspension (1 min – 28 days). Member is silenced across all channels.
Staff
/mod warn
Issue a formal warning — logged as a case and DM'd to the target operative.
Staff New
/mod purge
Bulk-delete 1–100 messages. Messages older than 14 days are automatically skipped.
Staff
/mod case
Retrieve a specific moderation case by its number in this server.
Staff New
/mod history
View the complete moderation history for any operative — up to 15 most recent cases.
Staff New

Public

Public New
💰 Fiscal Sector (Economy)
/economy daily
Claim your daily credit ration of 2,500 credits. Resets every 24 hours.
Public

Complete a tactical assignment for 250–750 credits. 1-hour cooldown between deployments.
Public New

High-risk extraction protocol. 70% chance of earning 500–2,500 cr, 30% chance of losing up to 1,000 cr. 30-min cooldown.
Public New

View your credit balance or check any other operative's fiscal status.
Public

Public

Global top-10 wealthiest operatives across all Astra-linked communities.
Public

Public New

Public New
🎫 Logistics Sector (Tickets)
/ticket create
Opens a private support channel visible only to you, staff, and Astra. One active ticket per user.
Public New
/ticket close
Close the current ticket. Channel is deleted after a 5-second grace period.
Public New

Grant a user view and write access to the current ticket channel.
Public New
/ticket remove
Revoke a user's access to the current ticket. Cannot remove the ticket creator.
Public New
