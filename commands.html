Every Astra command — what it does, who can use it, and whether it works in DMs.

DM ReadyEveryone

Call heads or tails. Win double your bet or lose it all. 50/50 odds.

🛡️ Moderation
/mod warn
Warn a user. A DM is sent to them with the reason. The case is saved in the database.
Staff
/mod kick
Kick a member from the server. Creates a case entry.
Staff
/mod ban
Permanently ban a user. Optionally delete their recent messages (0–7 days).
Staff
/mod unban
Remove an active ban by user ID. Creates an unban case.
Staff
/mod timeout
Timeout a member for 1 minute to 28 days. They cannot send messages or join voice during timeout.
Staff
/mod purge
Bulk-delete up to 100 messages in the current channel. Only deletes messages under 14 days old.
Staff
/mod case
View the details of a specific moderation case by its number.
Staff
/mod history
See a user's full moderation history — warns, bans, kicks, timeouts. Last 15 shown.
Staff
