PRIVACY POLICY

Archival protocols regarding the acquisition and persistence of operative data.

01 DATA ACQUISITION

Astra collects only the minimal telemetry required to perform its autonomous routines. This data is acquired directly from the Discord API during interaction phases.

Public Data

User IDs, Usernames, Guild IDs, and Role hierarchies.

System Data

Message timestamps, Command logs, and XP levels.

02 DATA PERSISTENCE

Operative data is persisted within an encrypted **PostgreSQL cluster**. This includes leveling matrices, economy balances, and active support tickets. We do not store message content or private communication.

03 USER RIGHTS

Operatives have the right to request a total wipe of their data from the Astra Intelligence Matrix. To initiate a **Right to Erasure**, join our Support Sector.
