PRIVACY POLICY

archival protocols regarding the acquisition and persistence of operative data.

01 DATA ACQUISITION

Astra collects only the minimal telemetry required to perform its autonomous routines. This data is acquired directly from the Discord API during interaction phases.

Public Data

User IDs, Usernames, Guild IDs, and Role hierarchies.

System Data

Message timestamps, Command execution logs, and XP levels.

02 DATA PERSISTENCE (POSTGRESQL)

Operative data is persisted within an encrypted **PostgreSQL cluster**. This includes your leveling matrix, economy balance, and active support tickets. We do not store message content, images, or sensitive personal information outside of active moderation logs.

03 VANGUARD ERROR REPORTING

In the event of a system anomaly, a diagnostic packet is transmitted to the Astra Core Team. This packet contains the Error Stack Trace and the Context ID. No private user communication is ever included in these reports.

04 USER RIGHTS & ERASURE

Citizens have the right to request a total wipe of their data from the Astra Intelligence Matrix. To initiate a **Right to Erasure**, join our Support Sector or use the `/utility request-wipe` protocol (where available).

05 THIRD-PARTY TRANSMISSION

Astra never sells or distributes operative data to third-party entities. All data remains within the Astra Secure Network for the sole purpose of guild moderation and utility.
