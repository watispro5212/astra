PRIVACY POLICY

How we handle and protect your data while using Astra.

01 DATA COLLECTION

Astra collects only the basic info required for the bot to work correctly. This data is gotten directly from Discord when you use the bot.

Public Data

User IDs, Usernames, Server IDs, and Roles.

System Data

Message timestamps, Command logs, and XP levels.

02 SAVING DATA

Your data is saved securely in our database. This includes your levels, money balances, and support tickets. While we don't store your chat messages, your AI settings (like which model you picked) are saved so you don't have to set them every time.

03 AI CHATS

Messages sent to the AI Assistant in DMs are processed by our AI systems. Astra does not store these chat logs in its database. However, messages are sent to our AI providers (like Google) to generate responses.

04 YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to request that all your data be deleted from Astra. To delete your data, please join our Support Server.
