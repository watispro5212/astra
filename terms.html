TERMS OF SERVICE

The simple rules for using the Astra bot.

01 ACCEPTANCE OF TERMS

By adding Astra to your server, you (the "User" or "Server Owner") agree to these rules. If you do not agree to these rules, you must remove Astra from your server.

02 PROPER USE

Astra is provided as a moderation and help tool. Users are not allowed to use Astra for spamming or any action that breaks the Discord Terms of Service.

03 DATA SAFETY

Astra uses a database to keep track of things like levels and money. While we try our best to keep everything safe, Astra is not responsible for data loss caused by Discord issues or technical glitches.

04 ERROR REPORTING

If Astra crashes, it might send a report to the developer. This info is only used to fix bugs and make the bot better. By using Astra, you agree to this.

05 AI ASSISTANT RULES

Users are not allowed to use the **AI Assistant** to generate prohibited content or try to break its safety filters. Breaking these rules will result in losing access to the AI features.
