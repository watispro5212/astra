v3.0 Feature Set

Every feature, documented

Astra v3 ships with 20+ modules covering everything your server needs.

🛡️ Moderation Suite

⚖️

Case Tracking

Every action (kick, ban, mute, warn) creates a numbered case. Full history, appeals, and CSV/JSON export.

🔇

Timeout / Mute

Native Discord timeouts with flexible duration parsing (10m, 1h, 2d). Fully reversible with /mod unmute.

⚠️

Warning System

Issue formal warnings, view warning history, remove individual warnings, or clear all at once. DM notification on warn.

New in v3
🔒

Channel Lock / Unlock

Lock any channel instantly to prevent member messages. Staff see a reason; lock persists until manually lifted.

New in v3
🧹

Purge & Slowmode

Bulk-delete up to 100 messages instantly. Set slowmode delays from 1 second to 6 hours per channel.

🤖

Auto-Moderation

Automatic spam, link, invite, caps, and bad-word filtering with dynamic custom rules per server.

🚨 Security & Anti-Raid

🛡️

Mass-Join Detection

Monitors join rate in real-time. Triggers automatic lockdown when join burst exceeds a configurable threshold.

New in v3
🔐

Automatic Lockdown

On raid detection, new members are auto-kicked until staff lifts the lockdown with /antiraid unlock.

New in v3
🔍

Permission Auditing

Compare two roles side-by-side or run a full guild permission audit to surface dangerous over-permissions.

💰 Economy System

🪙

Coins & Balance

Wallet + bank system. Earn coins through daily rewards, working, or admin grants. Transfer coins between members.

New in v3
🛒

Server Shop

Admins create shop items with prices and optional role rewards. Members spend coins to purchase items instantly.

New in v3
📈

Economy Leaderboard

Top 10 wealthiest members ranked by combined wallet + bank. Medal icons for the top 3 richest.

New in v3

🏆 Engagement & Leveling

✨

XP & Levels

Earn XP by chatting. Configurable rate and cooldown. Level-up announcements in a dedicated channel.

🎁

Giveaways

Button-based persistent giveaways with configurable duration, winner count, and automated winner announcements.

📊

Interactive Polls

Create timed and anonymous polls with up to 10 options. Live progress bars update as votes come in.

🌟 Community Features

💡

Suggestions

Members submit ideas with reaction voting. Staff approve, deny, or implement. Authors get DM status updates.

New in v3
🎂

Birthday System

Set your birthday, view upcoming birthdays, get auto-announced on your day, and receive an optional birthday role.

New in v3
💤

AFK System

Go AFK with a custom reason. Astra auto-replies when you're mentioned and removes AFK when you send a message.

New in v3
⭐

Starboard

Community highlights with configurable star threshold, self-star filtering, and media/image support.

🎭

Reaction Roles

Persistent button and dropdown role menus. Unique-role mode enforces mutual exclusivity within a menu.

📨

Invite Tracker

Track every invite: who invited who, total count, fake and leave tracking. Full invite leaderboard.

New in v3
