BOT FEATURES

Astra packs nine production-ready systems into a single free bot. Browse them all below, or filter by category to find what you need.

🛡️
Moderation

MODERATION

A complete toolkit for keeping your server safe. Every action is tracked with a unique case number, and users receive automatic DM notifications when moderated. Role hierarchy protection prevents junior staff from accidentally targeting senior members.

  • Ban, kick, warn, timeout — full suite of actions
  • Automatic DM notifications with reason included
  • Case system — full per-user action history
  • Purge up to 100 messages at once
  • Mod log channel for full audit trail
/moderation Wiki →

Economy

ECONOMY SYSTEM

A fully featured virtual economy where users earn money by working, gambling, mining, and claiming daily rewards. Balance is split into cash (raidable) and bank (safe). The economy is global — your balance is the same across every server.

  • Daily rewards with streak multipliers
  • Gamble, slots, coinflip, rob, work
  • Bank system — cash is raidable, bank is safe
  • Passive income via shop items + harvest
  • Pay friends, check balances, view leaderboard
/economy Wiki →
📈
Leveling

LEVELING SYSTEM

Members earn XP just by chatting in the server. An anti-spam cooldown ensures XP reflects genuine engagement. Level up to unlock role rewards. Compete on the leaderboard and flex your rank card. Each server has its own independent leaderboard.

  • XP earned by messaging — anti-spam cooldown built in
  • Level-up announcements in a configured channel
  • Admin-configured role rewards per level
  • Rank cards with XP progress bar
  • Server leaderboard — server-specific rankings
/leveling Wiki →

AI

AI ASSISTANT

Powered by free Google Gemini models. In DMs, just send Astra any message and the AI responds — no slash command needed. In server channels, use /ai chat. Multiple free models available. No rate limits, no paywalls, always on.

  • DM AI — no command needed, just message Astra
  • Powered by free Google Gemini models
  • Choose your preferred AI model
  • Conversational context — natural follow-ups
  • Available 24/7, free for all users

Community

GIVEAWAY ENGINE

Create timed giveaways in seconds. Members enter by clicking the reaction. When time runs out, Astra automatically selects random winners, announces them, and optionally pings them. Supports up to 10 winners per draw and a reroll command if needed.

  • Timed giveaways: 1m to 7d duration
  • Up to 10 winners per draw
  • Manual end and reroll with one command
  • Bot and alt account filtering
  • Live countdown displayed on giveaway embed

Economy+

STOCK MARKET

A simulated live stock market with 5 tradable companies. Prices fluctuate realistically using bull and bear cycles. Buy low, sell high, and track your portfolio — all using your virtual economy money. The market never sleeps.

  • 5 tradable stocks with live price updates
  • Buy and sell shares using economy money
  • Portfolio view with profit/loss tracking
  • Realistic bull and bear market simulation
  • Market view command shows all current prices

Support

SUPPORT TICKETS

Private per-user support channels automatically managed by Astra. Staff get instant access via their role. Transcripts are saved on close for a complete audit trail. Tickets keep support organized and private.

  • One private channel per user
  • Automatic staff role channel access
  • Add or remove users mid-ticket
  • Transcript saved to log channel on close
  • Confirmation step prevents accidental closure
/ticket Wiki →

Automation

WELCOME SYSTEM

Automatically greet every new member with a personalized message and optionally assign them a role. Use template variables like {user}, {server}, and {membercount} to make each message unique. Test it before going live.

  • Custom welcome messages with template variables
  • Auto-role assigned to every new member
  • Farewell messages when members leave
  • Test command to preview before going live
  • Toggle welcome and farewell independently
/welcome Wiki →

Economy+

SERVER SHOP

A per-server item store where members spend their economy money. Admins populate the shop with roles, consumables, and passive income items. Members buy, use, and sell back items. A great way to make the economy feel meaningful.

  • Role items that grant Discord roles on purchase
  • Passive income items harvested with /economy harvest
  • Consumable items (single use)
  • Sell-back items for 50% of original price
  • Full admin controls: add, edit, remove items
