v6.2.0 Core Features

Clean, Modern, Powerful

Astra v6 focuses on the essentials: professional management without the bloat.

🛡️ Professional Moderation

⚖️

Case Tracking

Every action (kick, ban, mute, warn) creates a numbered case. Full history lookup and easy appeals management.

🔇

Native Timeouts

Utilize Discord's native timeout system with flexible duration parsing (10m, 1h, 2d). Clean, reliable, and effective.

⚠️

Warning System

Issue formal warnings with automated DM notifications. Track member conduct with ease and precision.

🔒

Channel Controls

Lockdown channels instantly with /lock. Manage chat flow with /purge and /slowmode commands.

📋

Audit Logging

A comprehensive log of all server activity, from moderation actions to channel updates and member transitions.

🛡️

Permission Audit

Scan your server for dangerous permission configurations and compare roles side-by-side for perfect security.

🎫 Support & Tickets

🎫

Private Support

Launch dedicated support channels with one-click panels. Private, secure, and fully manageable by your staff.

👋

Staff Controls

Claim tickets, add/remove members, and close sessions with intuitive button-based interactions.

📄

HTML Transcripts

Generate beautiful, permanent archives of ticket conversations for record-keeping and accountability.

👋 Onboarding & Roles

✨

Guided Welcome

Create a perfect first impression with automated join messages and auto-role assignment for new members.

🎭

Role Menus

Professional button-based menus for self-assignable roles. Keep your server organized and interactive.

📜

Blueprint Setup

Build a complete, professional server structure in seconds with Astra's automated infrastructure builder.

⭐

Starboard
