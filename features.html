Features

Everything Astra brings to your server — professional, reliable, and powerful.

🛡️ Advanced Moderation

Auto-Moderation

Proactive protection against spam, links, invites, and excessive caps. Configurable filters that keep your server clean without manual intervention.

Timeouts & Bans

Remove disruptive members instantly with `/mute` (timeout), `/kick`, and `/ban`. Bans include a confirmation prompt to prevent accidents.

Case Management

Every action is logged with a unique case ID. Track warnings, mutes, and bans for every user with full accountability.

Detailed Logging

Real-time logging for message edits, deletions, and member events. Rich embeds provide full context for moderators.

💬 Community & Engagement

XP & Leveling

Reward active members with XP and levels. Configurable role rewards and automated level-up announcements.

Giveaways

Host persistent, button-based giveaways. Astra handles entries, timers, and winner selection automatically.

Interactive Polls

Timed and anonymous polls with real-time results. Up to 10 options per poll with beautiful button interfaces.

Starboard

Highlight the best content in your server. Messages that reach the star threshold are automatically featured in a dedicated channel.

⚙️ Tools & Utility

Ticket System

Advanced support tickets with transcripts. Categorize tickets (Support, Report, Inquiry) and log all closures to an audit channel.

Temporary Voice

"Join-to-Create" voice hubs. Users get their own temporary channel with controls to lock, unlock, and manage their space.

Server Stats

Detailed dashboards showing member growth, activity levels, and server metrics at a glance.

Welcome System

Customizable join and leave messages. Automatically assign roles to new members upon entry.

🔧 Utility

Reminders

Set personal reminders with flexible time formats like 10m, 1h, or 2d. Astra checks every minute and pings you in the channel when your reminder fires. Manage active reminders anytime.

Bot Info & Latency

Check Astra's response time with the ping command, which shows both gateway and REST latency. The about command gives you key info about the bot's framework and developer.
