Frequently Asked Questions

Mission-critical guidance for establishing your server infrastructure.

📊 Astra Intelligence (Leveling)

How does XP progression work?

Citizens earn 15-25 XP per transmission. To maintain data integrity, a 60-second activity cooldown is enforced between XP-earning interactions.

Are ranks global or server-specific?

XP and Levels are currently server-specific to ensure local community integrity, but global rank cross-referencing is planned for v6.4.0.

💰 Astra Fiscal (Economy)

How do I claim my daily credits?

Use the /economy daily command every 24 hours to receive your 500 credit allocation.

Can I transfer credits between users?

Yes, the /economy pay protocol allows for secure capital transfers between server operatives.

🏗️ Architect (Setup)

What is the "Nuclear Protocol"?

The Nuclear Protocol in /setup_server performs a total infrastructure purge before rebuilding your server according to Astra Prime blueprints. Use with caution.
